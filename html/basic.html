DAVID WARNER


David Andrew Warner (born 27 October 1986) is a Australian international cricketer and a former Test vice-captain. A left-handed opening batsman, Warner was the first Australian cricketer in 132 years to be selected for the national team in any format without experience in first-class cricket. He plays for New South Wales and Sydney Thunder in domestic cricket.
